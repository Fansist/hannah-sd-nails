The Studio

Celebrity-caliber artistry, one intricate set at a time

Hannah SD Nails is a luxury, appointment-only nail studio founded by a celebrity nail artist who has worked with Janet Jackson and Kali Uchis.

The studio is especially known for custom anime and 3D nail art, hand-painted designs, and editorial-level detail, alongside Japanese manicures and Dazzle Dry for clients who want natural-nail health and clean beauty.

Every appointment is treated as a canvas. With 1,200+ photos of finished sets and 55 Yelp reviews, the work speaks for itself.
